Code related to the AS-NMD paper

1. asenmd_analyses.Rmd & asenmd_analyses.html provide code for basic data processes & plots.
2. run_motif_enrich.R & motifEnrich.R provide for data process & plots of motif mapping to AS events, related to figure 2.
3. run_peak_enrich.R & peakEnrich.R provide for data process & plots of eclip peaks mapping to AS evnets, related to figure 2.
4. run_te_enrich.R & teEnrich.R provide for data process & plots of repetive elements to AS events, related to figure 3.
5. encode_rnaseq_rmats_code provide workflow for rna-seq analyses, related to figure 5 & 6.
